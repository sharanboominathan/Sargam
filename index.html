Our Story

From Village Youth to Master Craftsman

Prakash Sathiyadharan began his musical journey at age 16, dedicating 16 years to Svaram — Auroville's renowned musical instrument unit. His passion led him to earn a degree in West African drumming and perform internationally across Thailand and Vietnam.

Today, Sargam Musicals creates authentic sound healing instruments while teaching village youth the craft as a sustainable livelihood. Every instrument carries the spirit of Auroville's conscious living philosophy.
